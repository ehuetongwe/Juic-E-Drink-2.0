What's Inside & Why It Works:
Green Apple

Naturally sweet and full of fiber, green apples help your digestion, keep your blood sugar steady, and support a healthy gut.

Cucumber

Made mostly of water, cucumbers deeply hydrate your body and give your skin a healthy glow thanks to minerals like potassium.

Lemon & Key Lime

These citrus fruits help your body flush out toxins, support digestion, and bring balance to your system by reducing acidity.

Ginger

A warming root that's known for calming the stomach, boosting immunity, and reducing inflammation. It keeps your digestion moving and your body protected.

Juic'E Drinks operates on a limited batch pre order cycle. Orders are collected during a two week window. Production begins in week three, and all orders ship immediately after pressing to ensure peak freshness.

Once shipped, delivery typically takes 1 to 3 business days depending on your location.

All juices are frozen prior to shipment and packed inside insulated boxes with cold packs to maintain temperature during transit. This method helps ensure your juices arrive cold and ready to store.

If your juices arrive partially thawed but still cold, they are safe to refreeze or refrigerate.

Upon arrival, we recommend placing your juices in the freezer until you are ready to consume them.

Juices can be stored frozen for up to six months. Once thawed, keep refrigerated and consume within 3 to 5 days for optimal freshness.

Always keep juices refrigerated when not frozen.

For best results, transfer your juice from the freezer to the refrigerator 24 to 48 hours before you plan to drink it.

If you need to thaw more quickly, place the sealed bottle in a bowl of cool water. Avoid using hot water or microwaving, as this may affect quality.

Shake well before drinking, as natural separation may occur.
